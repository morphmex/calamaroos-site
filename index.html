Una distribuzione universale costruita su Gentoo Stage 3 + Systemd, con OpenRC in sviluppo, e l'installer grafico Calamares per rendere Gentoo accessibile a chiunque. E se questo non basta, una volta installato personalizzi tutto secondo le tue esigenze. A universal distribution built on Gentoo Stage 3 + Systemd, with OpenRC in development, and the Calamares graphical installer to make Gentoo accessible to everyone. Official binary packages, source compilation when needed, Flatpak integrated and the GURU community repository enabled by default. 7 desktop environments to choose from: the power of Gentoo, without barriers. And if that's not enough, once installed you can customize everything to your needs.
